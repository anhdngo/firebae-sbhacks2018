Who are we?

We are a team of UCSD students who believe that e-commerce can be much more than it currently is. Using our web development knowledge and innovative ideas, we have created a new system of e-commerce that exploits the utility of new tools such as FireBase to provide a better user and maintainer experience.

Learn More


Why choose us?

By using a FireBase database as our server, we are able to bring e-commerce fully to the cloud, reducing the complexity of development and maintenance. By consolidating our resources into one point of interaction, we can fully utilize many features that would have otherwise taken much more time to create, such as authentication, storage, hosting, messaging, and more!
